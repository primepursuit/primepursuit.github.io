Our Aims

We are an organization dedicated to provide original maths contents in the forms of articles, monthly problems, and contests.


We seek to promote creative thinking and logical reasoning, and most importantly, encourage more people to participate in problem curating. Other than writing articles and creating original problems, we also create animated videos explaining interesting and instructive problems on YouTube, highlighting specific techniques and noting creative ideas across various topics. We hope through this popular media that our work gets promoted, and our aims achieved.

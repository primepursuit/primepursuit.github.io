Events organized by Prime Pursuit:


P2SC Speed Challenge

The P2SC is a 2 hour long speed maths competition where you will be asked to find the answer of 20 interesting, beautiful and challenging problems. No proofs are required. The competition takes place betweeh the 13th and 17th May.


There will be both a senior (year 11-13 / grade 10-12) and junior event (year 10 or under / grade 9 or below under)


Scoring:

Questions 1-5: (1 mark)
Questions 6-10: (2 marks)
Questions 11-15: (3 marks)
Questions 16-20: (4 marks)


Registration: school must register through the google form https://forms.gle/RnzLjzDxQuJtaMeA7. Schools then choose a specific timeslot in which the paper and answer sheets would be emailed to the teacher in charge. Under this period students must be supervised under strict exam conditions. The teacher in charge then have to scan the students' answers.
